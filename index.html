I am a Research Associate in Statistics and Pathogen Phylodynamics at the Department of Mathematics at Imperial College London under the supervision of Oliver Ratmann. I work on HIV transmission and in particular I am focusing on determining the importance of risk factors in transmission network and the proportion of HIV introduction into a population by demographic characterics.
Before, I did my PhD in the IMAG lab at University of Montpellier under the supervision of Christophe Abraham, Pierre Pudlo and Meïli Baragatti. The title of my PhD thesis is Bayesian Functional Linear Regression and it is mainly about 1) to sparsely estimate the coefficient function of the Functional Linear Regression model and in particular its support, 2) to build an informative prior of this model from experts' knowledge and 3) to establish consistency of this model.
